About the UFA Simulator

The UFA Simulator is a platform for simulating real or custom Ultimate Frisbee games using detailed player stats, team data, and point-by-point logic based on the UFA (Ultimate Frisbee Association) season.

Whether you're a fan, analyst, or player, this tool gives you the power to explore how teams perform, compare players, and dive into the strategy behind every point.
